Welcome to Prevent a Stroke.org

Here at Prevent a Stroke.org, we pride ourselves in providing the highest quality information to the public about strokes. Our main focus (as you can probably guess) is preventing strokes. Our website is well equipped with all the informative material needed to help you and your loved ones live a life free of strokes.

You can calculate your risk of stroke in the calculator tab, and even quiz yourself in the quiz tab to ensure you've understood the information. You can also check out the news section for regular updates on strokes treatments and research. Make sure to create an account so that all the information we give you is personalised.

If there's on thing we can guarantee, Prevent a Stroke might be able to prevent you from having a stroke.

I hope you feel welcome here at the website, and that this information helps you in some way. Remember that healthiness is one step toward happiness, and as long as I get a paycheck at the end of the week, I don't care. Just use the website.
