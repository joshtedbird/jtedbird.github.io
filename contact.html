About Prevent-A-Stroke:

We are a non-profit organisation aimed at the prevention of strokes, saving not only lives but millions spent on treatment. We aim to make the public aware of the risk factors and their own level of risk but also letting them know how easy it is to better their chances of not having a stroke through simple changes of habit.
