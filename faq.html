Frequently Asked Questions


What is Prevent-A-Stroke?

We are a non-profit organisation aimed at promoting prevention over treatment and a nation-wide acknowledgement of Strokes and there causes.


How are you going to help me?

As well as providing a simple overview of what a stroke is, and how you can prevent it, our online calculator assesses your risks, and with login, we personalise information for you so you can take action and avoid a stroke!
Furthermore, our up to date newsfeed provides links to the most recent developments in research, including prevention, causes and treatments.


Why should I log in?

Logging in optimises your use of our on-site risk calculator and personalised information page. We give you personalised information based on the risk factors you’re currently engaging in as stated in the calculator questions (don’t worry your answers are safe and aren’t given out!) This means whenever you take the test again you can acknowledge changes you have made and watch your risk go down and feel better all at once.
