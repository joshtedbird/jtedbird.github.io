Privacy Statement

This privacy policy applies to all users visiting Prevent-A-Stroke.org, and reflects the way in which user information is collected, stored, and handled.

Collection of data

  • Prevent-A-Stroke only collects data through the optional creation of a user profile, or through the use of the online calculator.
  • Any stored data is kept securely on our database and can only be accessed by a user if they are logged into their profile.
  • Users are not able to access any other users' personal information - only their own.
  • Anonymous data may also be collected and stored should a user opt to use the risk calculator. Any lifestyle factors a user wishes to disclose may be stored anonymously.


    Use of personal information

  • Any personal details collected through the site is used solely for the purpose of creating a unique user profile for each registered member.No user details will be used by Prevent-A-Stroke besides for the purpose of creating said user profile. At Prevent-A-Stroke, some statistical information, collected through the risk calculator, may be analysed in order to draw conclusions and form statistics based upon the users of the site. All analytical data will remain anonymous.
  • If a user is logged in whilst participating in the risk calculator, their results will also be stored to their personal account so their profile may be customised to present them with the most relevant information to their circumstance. This data, however will remain anonymous should it be used for analytics at a later time. Only the user of whom the data belongs will be able to identify their personal results.


    Distribution of data

    No third party access to any user information is permitted. Prevent-a-stroke may utilise analysed anonymous data on the website to promote statistics or knowledge, however users will be unidentifiable and no user-specific references will be made nor implied. Users can enter their personal details and lifestyle information knowing their identity will remain hiden and secure at all times.


    What is deemed as 'Personal Information'?

    At Prevent-A-Stroke, we classify personal information as any characteristics or attributes of a user which may be used to specify their identity.
    These attributes include:

  • Name: given and family
  • Date of Birth: dd/mm/yyyy
  • Username: created upon account registration
  • Password: created upon account registration
  • Email address

    Lifestyle information collected through the "Risk Calculator" is not deemed 'personal' by Prevent-A-Stroke, and may be used in the analytics of the organisation; user anonymity will be upheld.
